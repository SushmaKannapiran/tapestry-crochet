    • 8. Why It Still Matters

    1. Market value: 300K-500K active crocheters spending Rs 2,000-5,000/year on yarn = Rs 60-250 crore market for crochet yarn alone.

    2. Growth rate: The market is growing 20-30% year-on-year (post-COVID explosion hasn't slowed down).

    3. Conversion opportunity: The traditional knitting base (2-3M people) can be converted to crochet with the right content and products.

    4. Proof of concept: Magic Needles built Rs 8.5 crore revenue from Rs 1 lakh investment serving this market — and they're just ONE brand.

    5. Global multiplier: Export multiplies the addressable market by 10x+. The US alone has 45 million knitters/crocheters. An Indian brand producing at Indian costs can compete globally at unbeatable prices.
